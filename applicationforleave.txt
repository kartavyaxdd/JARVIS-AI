Here is a sample application for leave:

**Application for Leave**

**To,**
[Supervisor's Name]
[Designation]
[Company Name]
[Date]

**Subject: Request for Leave from [Date] to [Date]**

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from [Date] to [Date] due to [briefly mention the reason for leave, e.g., personal reasons, family emergency, illness, etc.].

I have made sure to complete all my tasks and ensure that my work is up to date before applying for this leave. I will be available by email if anything urgent comes up during my absence.

I would like to request your permission to grant me this time off. I will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I return.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

**Your Name**
[Your Designation]

Note: You can customize this application as per your needs and requirements.
